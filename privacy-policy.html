Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information through the ProbetIQ app. We are committed to ensuring compliance with applicable privacy regulations, including the CCPA, GDPR, and COPPA.

Information We Collect

Personal Information Collected

  • Contact Information: We collect information such as email addresses, phone numbers, and unique identifiers to manage user accounts, facilitate communications, and provide services.
  • Location Data: Our app may collect location data to provide personalized experiences and recommendations.

Permissions

To deliver optimal service, we request the following permissions:

  • Push Notifications: To provide timely updates, reminders, and alerts.
  • Contact Information: Specifically, your phone number, email address, and identification for each user to enhance account security and support.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Deliver services and personalize your experience.
  • Communicate with you through notifications.
  • Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements.
  • Improve and monitor app performance.

Data Collection Through Third-Party APIs

Our app interacts with third-party services to provide real-time data and analytics. We may share data with the following endpoints as part of the service:

  • API Football: For delivering sports-related data.
  • Rapid API: For data integrations and content delivery.
  • ProbetIQ Hosted Endpoints: Including https://probetiq.github.io and https://be-probetiq.onrender.com for data processing, storage, and user account management.

Each of these services adheres to its own privacy standards, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Data Security and Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. All data transfers and storage comply with industry standards, including encryption where applicable.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have specific rights regarding your personal data, such as the right to access, update, or delete information. For further details, refer to the applicable regulation section (e.g., GDPR, CCPA, COPPA).

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or relevant regulations. We encourage users to review this document regularly.
